# PRD P6 — AI Video Advanced Controls

> **Master Plan**: [plan-video-support.md](./plan-video-support.md)
> **Priority**: P6 — Growth / Differentiation
> **Area**: App (Frontend)

---

## Objective

Expose meaningful but not overwhelming control surface for AI video generation.

---

## Controls

- **Quality**: Low / Standard / High
- **Negative Prompt** (optional)
- **Sound**: on / off
- **Enhance**: on / off

---

## Guardrails

- Collapse under "Advanced" section
- Clear cost indication if higher quality increases compute cost

---

## Success Metrics

- % users engaging advanced controls
- Compare regenerate rate before/after
